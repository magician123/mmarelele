What We Do

Mmarelele was registered in 2016 specifically to deal with building and Engineering projects. it was initially established in 2000 as an ad hoc and service provider for electrical engineering project and consulting practise, however due to the changing built environment and the growing needs of our clients; it became necessary to broaden the services and incorporate a lot of service offerings like construction, earth works and landscaping

We strive to connect as much as we can with our clients and build relationships. at Mmarelele we hire specialts for the respective domain and that results in quality service. Mmarelele is 100% black owned and level 1 B-BBEE company.

Mmarelele has continued to strive towards becoming a complete multi-disciplinary practise offering our clients the specialist individual attention and solutions based on their requirements. Mmarelele Pty Ltd prides itself with high quality of teamwork, integrity, accountability and speedy delivery on every project. Mmarelele believes that professionalism and quality service is the key to success of any project.
